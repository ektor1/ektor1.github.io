About me

I was originally born and grew up in Greece until I left to study Finance & Business at the University of Sussex. During my undergrad I did a short internship at the prime brokerage office of Cowen Inc. in London. After that, I went to Sofia, Bulgaria to work for a few months in commercial banking at ProCredit Bank. Eventually I quit my job to pursue my MSc in Business Analytics at UCL. I finished my master's program in August 2022 and I am currently based in London.
